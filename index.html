Our Story

The Partner Behind the Brand

Founded in 2006, Ealytex Global Inc. is a U.S.-based fashion manufacturer headquartered in the heart of New York City's garment district. We operate as a fully integrated partner — bringing design, development, and production together to support brands from concept through execution.

Over the years, we've partnered with some of the most recognized names in global retail including Costco, Walmart, Amazon, and Target.

As niche-focused marketers with hands-on design and development expertise, we move quickly to identify emerging trends and strategically align product assortments to evolving consumer demand.

What We Do

End-to-End Services

01
Design & Development
Market research, material sourcing, fabric innovation, trim development, and customized design packages. Including 3D garment visualization to create commercially viable designs that balance creativity, performance, and profitability.
02
Sourcing & Production
Deep expertise across denim, sweaters, cut-and-sew knits, seamless, activewear, lingerie, and wovens. We source high-quality fabrics and trims that meet strict standards — delivering consistent quality at competitive pricing.
03
Shipping & Logistics
Fast, accurate, and reliable delivery. We coordinate direct shipment to warehouses or distribution centers and manage the entire logistics process — from factory floor to final destination — to ensure on-time delivery.
