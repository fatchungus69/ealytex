Our Story

The Partner Behind the Brand

Founded in 2016, Ealytex Global Inc. is a U.S.-based fashion manufacturer headquartered in the heart of New York City's garment district. We operate as a fully integrated partner — bringing design, development, and production together to support brands from concept through execution.

Over the years, we've partnered with some of the most recognized names in global retail including Costco, Walmart, Amazon, and Target.

As niche-focused marketers with hands-on design and development expertise, we move quickly to identify emerging trends and strategically align product assortments to evolving consumer demand.
